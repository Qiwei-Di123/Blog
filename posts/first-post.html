My First Blog

February 17, 2026

Welcome to my blog! This is my first post. My goal is to share my personal understanding of various topics in a vivid and engaging writing style.

Why I Started This Blog

I'm not a very smart person. Whenever I learn something, I tend to forget it soon after, especially when I move on to other subjects. It's a pity that so many thoughts interesting or not get lost along the way. One solution is to write notes, which can organize ideas in a logical manner. But I want to do something more interesting, and more challenging.

What You Can Expect

I will write stories about what I've learned. I'll start in Chinese and use AI to help polish my sentences or translate them into English when needed. If I learn other languages later, I may also use AI to write in those languages.

About AI

I greatly appreciate the help of AI. I couldn't do this without it. While AI-generated writing still has its limitations, it helps me tremendously in expressing my thoughts.
